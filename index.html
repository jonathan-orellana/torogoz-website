Named for a symbol of freedom

The torogoz — the turquoise-browed motmot — is the national bird of El Salvador, a symbol of freedom, family, and resilience across Latin America. Our chapter carries its name as a reminder of where we come from and the heritage we proudly represent on Grounds.
